# Wrathbound Soldier

_Medium Humanoid, Chaotic Evil_

---

**Armor Class** 11  
**Hit Points** 22 (5d8+2)  
**Speed** 30 ft.

---

|   STR   |   DEX   |   CON   |   INT   |   WIS   |   CHA   |
| :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: |
| 15 (+2) | 11 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 11 (+0) |

---

**Skills** Intimidation +2  
**Senses** Passive Perception 10  
**Languages** Common  
**Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)  
**Proficiency Bonus** +2

---

## Traits

_**Pack Tactics.**_ The soldier has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the soldier's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The soldier makes two mace attacks.

_**Mace.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. _Hit:_ 5 (1d6+2) bludgeoning damage.

_**Heavy Crossbow.**_ _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +2 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. _hit:_ 5 (1d10) piercing damage.
