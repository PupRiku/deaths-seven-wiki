# Fizzle

_Tiny Fey (Pixie), Chaotic Good_

---

**Armor Class** 15  
**Hit Points** 20 (8d4)  
**Speed** 10 ft., fly 40 ft.

---

|  STR   |   DEX   |  CON   |   INT   |   WIS   |   CHA   |
| :----: | :-----: | :----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: |
| 2 (-4) | 20 (+5) | 8 (-1) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 15 (+2) |

---

**Skills** Perception +4, Stealth +5  
**Senses** Passive Perception 14  
**Languages** Common, Sylvan  
**Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)  
**Proficiency Bonus** +2

---

## Traits

_**Innate Spellcasting.**_ The pixie's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring only its pixie dust as a component:
At will: druidcraft  
1/day each: confusion, dancing lights, detect evil and good, detect thoughts, dispel magic, entangle, fly, phantasmal force, polymorph, sleep

_**Magic Resistance.**_ Fizzle has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

## Actions

_**Superior Invisibility.**_ As an action, Fizzle magically turns invisible until its concentration ends (as if concentrating on a spell). Any equipment the pixie wears or carries is invisible with it.
